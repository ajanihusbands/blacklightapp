Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Freedom Units ("the App") is operated by Blacklight App LLC. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Freedom Units Pro") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

AI Conversions

When you use Freedom Units mode, your input is sent to a serverless API which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate conversions. Inputs are not stored or logged by us. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API requests.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Data Storage

Any data generated by the App (such as conversion history) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
