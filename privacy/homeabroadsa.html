Home Abroad SA

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Home Abroad SA ("the App") is a housing cost calculator for U.S. expats and other U.S. buyers exploring property purchases in South Africa. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or other personal details.

Information you enter into the calculator, such as purchase price, available funds, selected region, lender assumptions, estimated costs, and related inputs, is used only to calculate and display results within the App.

Data Storage

Any calculator inputs, preferences, saved settings, or generated estimates are stored locally on your device only, if stored at all. We do not have access to this information and it is not transmitted to our servers.

Financial, Tax, Legal, and Mortgage Information

Home Abroad SA provides educational estimates only. The App may include information about South African buying costs, transfer duty, bond or mortgage estimates, exchange-rate assumptions, and potential U.S. funding sources such as cash savings, 401(k) loans, Roth IRA contributions, and other savings.

The App does not provide tax, legal, mortgage, investment, retirement, or financial advice. Results are estimates and may not reflect your actual costs, eligibility, tax treatment, lender terms, exchange rates, or legal obligations. You should consult qualified professionals before making property, tax, retirement-account, financing, or investment decisions.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases or premium features processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's privacy and payment policies.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Home Abroad SA does not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple's App Store services to process in-app purchases and manage purchase status. We do not receive your full payment information from Apple.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
